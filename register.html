Regional Science Fair

The regional science fair will take place from Monday April 5, 2021 to Friday April 9, 2021. Up to 10 projects from grades 6, 7, and 8 may be selected to represent Florida Ruffin Ridley School.

If your research involves human subjects and you would like to represent the Florida Ruffin Ridley School at the regional fair, you will be required to have informed consent forms from your subjects. The regional fair has strict safety requirements that exclude the use of infectious agents, recombinant DNA and hazardous compounds. Additional information can be found here, or by talking with your science teacher, or asking Chris Dulla.
