Calling all scientists!

The theme of this year's fair is "Climate Change and Green Technology", and the fair is on Sunday, May 15th at 9AM in the FRR School cafeteria. All students, pre-K through 8th grade, are encouraged to participate.

Fair Date/Time: May 15, 2022 at 9:00 AM

Due to uncertainties surrounding the pandemic, the date, format, and location (virtual/indoors/outdoors) of fair is subject to change. The PTO will announce any changes in fair date and location in the coming weeks.

Please note: your project does not have to be about Climate Change and Green Technology. Your project can be about whatever you are excited about!

How to make a great science fair project

  • Find any topic that interests you
  • Ask a question
  • Make a prediction
  • Do an experiment to answer your question
  • Write down the results
  • Make a poster to share your project
  • Review our 2021 Science Fair Information Packet

Remember: plan a safe experiment, free from extreme hazards, explosives, and infectious agents.
